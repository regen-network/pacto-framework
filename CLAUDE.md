## What PACTO Is

PACTO (**Participatory Assembly & Co-creation Through Technology**) is a framework for generating durable, community-authored ecological agreements using participatory assembly processes augmented by AI tooling.

PACTO is a companion to [`regen-network/agentic-tokenomics`](https://github.com/regen-network/agentic-tokenomics), extending it for participatory contexts.

## Core Thesis

Communities already have governance processes and cultural decision-making rituals. Rather than replacing those with outside legal and technical frameworks, PACTO instruments existing assembly processes with technology that makes them more legible, actionable, and contractually binding — while keeping the community's voice as the primary source of authority.

## The Four Problems

1. **The Implementation Gap** — Consent is often reduced to procedural minimum compliance rather than the substantive participatory right governance principles intend. PACTO provides methodology for full implementation.
2. **Organizational Capture** — External organizations design projects without community input, creating fragmented, funding-dependent dynamics.
3. **The Accessibility Challenge** — Meaningful participation requires sustained engagement. AI-augmented synthesis makes intensive assembly sessions more productive, increasing depth per session rather than compressing timelines.
4. **The Surveillance Inversion** — Most conservation technology watches communities. PACTO inverts this.

## The 6-Step Core Loop

1. **Community Assembly** — convenes using existing governance processes
2. **Voice-to-Text Capture** — assembly proceedings transcribed
3. **AI-Assisted Synthesis** — themes, priorities, agreements, tensions extracted
4. **Feedback Presentation** — community reviews, corrects, affirms
5. **Iterative Refinement** — repeat until community consensus
6. **Agreement Generation** — formal legal instruments rooted in community voice

## Cultural Sensitivity Guidelines

**Mandatory when working with PACTO content:**

1. **Respect oral tradition as primary source.** Voice is the primary interface, not a fallback.
2. **Be aware of power dynamics.** AI synthesis is always subject to community correction.
3. **Preserve language integrity.** Flag potential semantic loss in translation.
4. **No extraction without reciprocity.** Contributions must add value for communities.
5. **Present the current state honestly.** Do not overstate maturity.
6. **Legal pluralism awareness.** Flag legal complexity rather than simplifying it away.
7. **Frame accessibility, not efficiency.** PACTO's value is depth of engagement per session, not timeline compression or cost reduction.
